Who We Are

Rooted in Quality, Driven by Purpose

At Southern Agriculture Irrigation, LLC, we specialize in the intersection of robust wood manufacturing and agricultural necessity. Located in Pinetta, Florida, our facility transforms raw timber into essential structural components that support irrigation systems and farming logistics.

We understand that agricultural environments are harsh and demanding. That is why our wood products are treated and processed to withstand humidity, soil contact, and heavy industrial use. From custom crating to structural supports for irrigation lines, our craftsmanship ensures longevity.

Our philosophy is simple: sustainable sourcing combined with precision manufacturing creates products that farmers and industries can rely on for decades.

Our Capabilities

Specialized Production

Irrigation Structural Supports

We manufacture heavy-duty wooden stakes, bracing, and support blocks specifically designed to stabilize above-ground irrigation piping. Our treated wood resists rot and water damage, ensuring the integrity of your water delivery systems in the field.

Custom Agricultural Crating

Secure your harvest and equipment with our bespoke crate and pallet solutions. We design wood packaging that optimizes airflow for produce while providing the structural rigidity needed for safe transport and heavy stacking.

Milled Timber Products

Beyond specialized supports, we offer precision milling for general agricultural construction. Whether you need fencing posts, barn siding, or utility poles, our facility delivers consistent dimensions and high-grade lumber.
